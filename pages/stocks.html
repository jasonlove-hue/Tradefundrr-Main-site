Express Funding · the full breakdown

Your Express Funding Account Details

Simple. Transparent. No hidden rules. No hidden fees. Click any row to expand. First row is open by default.

▾ Account Size $25,000

Get instant access to a $25,000 funded account, offering immediate trading power and flexibility to capitalize on market opportunities.

Which means: you start with a $25,000 funded account size, with a path to scale up through performance. No waiting on slow evals.

▾ Intraday Buying-Power $100,000

Intraday buying-power is 4× the account balance. Example: if your account balance is $25,000, you can trade shares worth $100,000.

Symbol/Ticker Limit: you are not allowed to use more than 25% of your account's buying-power in one symbol/ticker at a time. Example: even though you have $100,000 intraday buying-power, you can only trade a maximum of $25,000 worth of one symbol/ticker at any given time.

Which means: you can put $100,000 of stock to work intraday on $25K of account, but no single ticker can absorb more than $25K of that buying power.

▾ Daily Loss Limit $1,000

Maximum amount you are allowed to lose in one trading day. This is a soft breach and will disable your account for the duration of the session.

Which means: one rough day won't blow the whole account. If you hit -$1,000 in a session, the rest of the day is paused; you reset for the next.

▾ Max End of Day Drawdown $3,750

The maximum amount you are allowed to lose from your peak account equity. Calculated at the end of each trading day.

Which means: your account is protected by a trailing drawdown calculated on your highest end-of-day equity. Keep your peak-to-trough swing inside $3,750 and you keep trading.

▾ Weekly Payouts Unlocked At Profit Target $1,250

Unlock your payout privileges to start requesting weekly payouts from your Express Funding account.

To unlock your payout privilege you need to reach the profit target of your account, while showing consistent profit-taking skills on 5 trading days (non-consecutive) with a minimum daily profit of $250 on each of the 5 days.

As soon as your payout privileges are unlocked you can request an immediate payout of 50% of your profits.

To unlock permanent unlimited 100% profit payouts you need to complete 6 payout cycles (each cycle requires 5 non-consecutive trading days of $250 or more, and that the profit target has been reached).

Once your 100% profit payouts are unlocked, you keep this privilege and can request 100% payouts of your profits on a weekly basis.

Keep in mind: we aim to move traders to our live pro trade desk as soon as we observe strong performance and risk-management skills. Once you are moved to our live desk, all payout restrictions are removed, and you can request 100% of your profit share without limitations.

Which means: you don't have to hit some massive target before getting paid. $1,250 of profit plus 5 disciplined trading days unlocks your first 50% payout; six clean cycles unlock unlimited 100%.

▾ Drawdown High Water Mark $25,000

Once your trailing drawdown matches your initial balance, it will no longer trail and will be locked in at that level for the duration of the evaluation. This allows you to establish a cushion and enjoy a higher maximum drawdown, providing greater flexibility in managing your trades.

Which means: once your trailing drawdown reaches your starting balance, it locks. Every dollar of profit after that becomes additional cushion, not pressure.

▾ Profit Split 80/20

You keep 80 percent of the earnings, we keep 20 percent. The profit split is the same across all of TradeFundrr's accounts (Growth, Express, Pro).

Which means: you keep 80 cents of every dollar of profit. No tiered scale, no qualifications. Same split on Growth, Express, and Pro.

▾ Payout Cycle Weekly

As soon as you're eligible for payouts, you can request weekly payouts of your profit split.

Which means: weekly payout requests, paid against simulated trading performance, deposited to your Rise account.

▾ Min Payout Amount $250

$250 is the minimum payout you can request at any weekly payout cycle.

The trader's dictionary.

Plain-English definitions for every term used on this page. Click any term to expand:

Eval / Evaluation▾
A paid trial period. You buy access to a simulated account, trade under published rules, and "pass" the evaluation by hitting the profit target without violating the loss limits. Express Funding skips the eval, you start trading day one.
Simulated capital▾
The $100K in the account is simulated, so you're not risking your savings to access it. The payouts earned against that simulated trading are real money, paid weekly to your Rise account.
Drawdown / Max drawdown▾
The peak-to-trough loss in your account. Max drawdown is the largest loss allowed before the account is suspended. The trailing drawdown adjusts upward as you earn; once it reaches the high-water mark, it locks at your starting balance.
PDT rule (Pattern Day Trader)▾
The old SEC rule requiring $25,000 minimum equity to day-trade four or more round trips in a five-business-day rolling window. It has since been repealed and no longer restricts retail or funded day trading.
PFOF (Payment for Order Flow)▾
When a retail broker sells your order flow to a market maker, who pays the broker for it. The market maker fills your order, often with a spread slightly worse than direct institutional routing. Most retail commission-free brokers use PFOF; TradeFundrr's funded accounts don't.
Sharpe ratio▾
A measure of risk-adjusted return. A high Sharpe means strong returns relative to volatility, i.e. the trader makes money without huge swings. T3 Global considers Sharpe (alongside other metrics) when reviewing TradeFundrr graduates for live capital.
Profit split▾
The percentage of profits you keep. TradeFundrr's split is 80/20: you keep 80%, the firm keeps 20%. Same across Growth, Express, and Pro.
Buying power▾
The dollar value of stock you can hold intraday. A $25,000 account balance with 4× leverage gives you $100,000 of intraday buying power. Capped at 25% per single ticker.
Audition path / Graduation▾
The process by which a TradeFundrr trader earns a real-capital seat at T3 Global. Triggered by sustained Sharpe, disciplined drawdown, and consistent profitability over multiple months. Not automatic; reviewed and approved on a case-by-case basis.

Does the PDT rule still apply?▾

No. The Pattern Day Trader rule was repealed and no longer restricts day trading frequency on TradeFundrr funded accounts. Which means: you can trade every day without worrying about the old four-day-rolling-window restriction that capped retail stock day traders.

Payouts run weekly. Once you're eligible, request a payout and approved requests process on Fridays. Earliest payout on Express Funding is 5 days from start. Which means: weekly payout windows, paid direct to your Rise account, performance-based not on-demand.

What if I blow the account?▾

If you exceed the drawdown limit, the account is suspended per the published program rules. You can purchase another evaluation or Express account to restart. Which means: the cost of failure is the eval fee, not your savings.

What platforms can I trade on?▾

DXTrade for most traders (built by the same team behind tastyworks and thinkorswim). Deep Charts for traders who want professional charting and one-click execution from the chart. Which means: the execution stack matches what a professional desk runs, not a retail brokerage.

Yes, but it's earned. Top-performing traders with sustained Sharpe and risk discipline audition for T3 Global's institutional trading desk.
