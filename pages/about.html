TradeFundrr was assembled differently. Christian took the partnership-first route: secure a regulated broker-dealer (T3 Trading Group, FINRA / SIPC), tie into a real institutional desk (T3 Global), then layer the trader-facing offer on top. The infrastructure came first. The marketing came second.

Which means the rules on the offer page are the actual rules. The payouts come out of real institutional infrastructure. And the graduation path to live capital is not a placeholder, it is the desk behind the offer.
