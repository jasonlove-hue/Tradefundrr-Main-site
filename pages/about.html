TradeFundrr was founded by Christian Bose, a trader who had spent years inside the financial markets and understood what serious traders actually needed to perform at their best: real capital, fair rules, and a firm they could trust to pay them consistently. the rules

Every rule explained.
Every rule visible before you buy.

TradeFundrr's Rules Exist To Protect Your Payouts, Not To Catch You Out. Here's Every One Of Them…

rule why it exists
Daily Loss Limit

Confirmed value

Protects your account from a single bad session wiping out multiple days of gains. A daily cap on losses means one difficult trading day doesn't end your funded account, it's a safeguard, not a penalty.
Maximum Drawdown

Confirmed value

Defines the total loss threshold for the account. Drawdown limits exist in every professional trading environment. This one ensures the capital behind your account is protected and that payouts remain sustainable for all funded traders.
Profit Target

Confirmed value

Defines the performance threshold for payout eligibility. The profit target isn't arbitrary, it's the benchmark that confirms a trader has demonstrated a genuine, repeatable edge rather than reaching profitability through a single large position or outlier trade.
Minimum Trading Days

Confirmed value

Sets a floor on how quickly positions can be opened and closed. This exists to ensure funded trading reflects genuine market engagement rather than exploiting very short-term inefficiencies that don't apply in institutional trading environments.
News Trading Rules

The minutes around major news releases can produce price action that no risk management framework can reliably contain. This rule protects funded traders from breaching their loss limits through events outside their control, and protects TradeFundrr's ability to process payouts reliably.

No hidden rules, no gotchas. Every TradeFundrr rule is visible before you buy, and every one of them is listed on the individual program pages…
