Implementation Notes

  1. Max Drawdown — EOD trailing. Calculated end of day. Stops trailing once the account reaches the initial balance threshold. The drawdown is fixed at that level afterwards; no further trailing.
  2. 2 Soft → Hard breach (crypto). First and second daily-loss breaches: account pauses, session ends, account resumes the next trading day. Third daily-loss breach: immediate failure (hard breach), account closed. Two warnings per account.
  3. Profit Cap (funded). Per-session cap. If a single trading session generates more than the profit cap, surplus is removed (similar to consistency-rule trim) or session is auto-flat. Confirm with backend team which behavior to implement.
  4. Consistency rule (funded only). No single trading session may exceed 30% of the running profit target. If exceeded, the profit target is recalculated upward to keep the trader within the consistency band.
  5. Profit split. 80% to trader / 20% to firm. The source spec also mentions "Payout: 50% of Gains" — this is currently ambiguous. Clarify with Jason whether it overrides the 80/20 split during a specific window (e.g. first payout) or refers to a different metric (e.g. account-size threshold for going live).
  6. Min trading days. 1 day for eval, 10 days for funded. "Days" — clarify whether calendar days or trading days. Current assumption: calendar days for the 10-day elapse window, but verify in backend rule definition before launch.
  7. Algo trading. Not permitted on any program. Backend must enforce manual-trade-only flag and reject API/algo order tags.
  8. Payout cycle — close day & cutoff. Weekly cadence is locked, but which day the cycle closes (Friday close? Sunday EOD?) and what time (UTC? ET?) are not yet specified. Backend cron + Rise settlement integration both need this defined before launch. Confirm with Jason.
