Your Questions, Answered Directly.

Is TradeFundrr a real prop firm?

Yes. TradeFundrr provides traders with access to simulated evaluation and funding-style programs designed to identify disciplined traders and support long-term development.

Is this simulated trading or live capital?

TradeFundrr programs operate in a simulated trading environment. Traders who demonstrate consistency and strong risk management may become eligible for additional funding opportunities based on internal evaluation criteria.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. All pricing and program costs are clearly displayed before checkout. Depending on the platform or market selected, certain standard data or platform fees may apply where applicable.

What makes TradeFundrr different from other prop firms?

TradeFundrr focuses on transparency, trader development, straightforward rules, and flexible platform access. Our goal is to create an environment where traders can focus on execution and risk management without unnecessary complexity.

What countries are restricted from joining?

Due to compliance and operational limitations, TradeFundrr is unable to provide services in certain countries and regions. Please review our restricted countries list before signing up.

Do you offer real trader support?

Yes. TradeFundrr provides onboarding resources, platform setup guides, dashboard access, and responsive customer support to help traders throughout their journey.

What markets can I trade?

Depending on your selected program, TradeFundrr offers access to Futures, Stocks, and Options markets.

What platforms do you use?

TradeFundrr supports multiple trading platforms, including TradingView, Tradovate Prop, NinjaTrader Prop, Quantower, and our web-based trading platform.

Can I trade with my own strategy and risk style?

Yes. Traders are free to use their own trading strategies as long as they follow the program’s risk management rules and prohibited trading practices.

Can I use bots or automated strategies?

Certain automated strategies and trade management tools may be permitted depending on the platform and program. Traders should always review the program rules before using automation.

Can I trade during news events?

Yes, unless otherwise specified in your program rules. Traders are responsible for managing risk during periods of increased market volatility.

Can I hold trades overnight or over the weekend?

Holding rules may vary depending on the market and program selected. Please review your specific account rules for overnight and weekend holding policies.

Are there minimum trading days?

Some programs may require a minimum number of trading days before completion or payout eligibility. Program requirements are always listed before purchase.

Is there a time limit to pass the evaluation?

Program timelines may vary depending on the account type. Certain programs may offer flexible timelines, while others include completion requirements.

What happens after I pass?

Once you successfully complete the program requirements, you may become eligible for a funded or simulated funded account, depending on the program structure and internal review process.

How do payouts work?

Eligible traders can request payouts directly through their dashboard. Approved payouts are processed through our payout provider according to the program’s payout schedule and requirements.

How are payouts processed?

Payout requests are securely processed through our payout partner. First-time users may be required to complete identity verification before receiving funds.

How many accounts can I have?

The number of accounts allowed may vary by program and current policy. Please review the account limits listed during signup or contact support for details.

What happens if I fail the evaluation?

If your account violates the program rules, the account will be closed. Depending on the program, you may have the option to reset or purchase a new evaluation account.

Can I reset or retry my account?

Yes. Traders who do not complete an evaluation successfully may have the option to reset or restart depending on the program selected.

How quickly can I start trading?

Most traders can begin trading shortly after completing signup, platform setup, and any required data agreements.

Can beginners apply?

Yes. TradeFundrr is designed for traders at different experience levels, from newer traders to experienced market participants.

Do I need prior trading experience to get started?

No prior professional trading experience is required. However, understanding basic market and risk management concepts is strongly recommended.

What kind of support do traders receive?

TradeFundrr provides onboarding resources, platform setup guides, dashboard access, and customer support to help traders navigate the program experience.
