Your Questions, Answered Directly.

Is TradeFundrr a real prop firm?

TradeFundrr is a simulated trading program designed to help traders develop their skills and progress toward funding opportunities. Traders operate in a structured simulated environment with clearly defined rules, risk parameters, and performance objectives. Eligible traders may qualify for payouts based on program performance and may become eligible for advancement into live funded opportunities where applicable.

How do payouts work?

Eligible traders can request payouts directly through the TradeFundrr dashboard once payout requirements are met. Payouts are processed through Rise, our payout partner, and are reviewed according to program guidelines and risk management standards. Weekly payout cycles are available for qualifying accounts.

Are the trading rules fair? Are there hidden rules?

TradeFundrr focuses on transparency and clearly defined trading rules. All account parameters, drawdown limits, payout requirements, and risk guidelines are outlined upfront so traders understand exactly what is required. Our goal is to create a structured environment built around consistency and long-term trader development.

What happens if I fail the evaluation?

If an evaluation account breaches the program rules, the account becomes inactive. Traders may choose to restart with a new evaluation account and begin again. Many successful traders require multiple attempts while refining their strategy, discipline, and risk management approach.

What platforms do you use?

TradeFundrr supports multiple professional trading platforms depending on the asset class and program selected. Supported platforms include TradingView, Tradovate Prop, NinjaTrader Prop, Quantower, DX Trade, DeepCharts and the TradeFundrr Web Platform. Platform availability may vary by Futures, Stocks, or Options programs.

Can I trade my existing strategy?

Yes. Traders are welcome to use their own trading strategies as long as they follow the program rules and risk parameters. TradeFundrr is designed to support a wide range of trading styles while encouraging consistency, discipline, and proper risk management.

What support do I get as a funded trader?

Funded traders receive access to platform support, account assistance, payout guidance, and educational resources designed to help traders navigate the program successfully. Our support team is available to assist with technical questions, platform setup, and account-related inquiries.
